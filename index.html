All wire manufactured by SMA Devices is made from alloy certified to meet the ASTM F2063 standard making it suitable for use for medical devices and surgical implants.

Depending on the application, SMA Devices can design wire so that it’s ready for -10ºC or 100ºC or anywhere in between. Clients specify their desired operating temperature and how the material should behave, and our team can suggest the appropriate alloy required. Nitinol wire is useful not just in superelastic applications, but for those that require thermal control, such as switches and actuators.

We can offer wire cut to a specific length, with or without radiused ends.
